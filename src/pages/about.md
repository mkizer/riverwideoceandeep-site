![Where The River Meets The Ocean (Michael Schauer)](/MichaelSchauer_WhereTheRiverMeetsTheOcean.png)
Where The River Meets The Ocean ~ [Michael Schauer](https://michaelschauer.com/)

I've always felt that the marriage of music and lyrics can be a powerful combination, so this site will dive into many of my favorites.

The title of the site comes from the [Fates Warning](http://fateswarning.com/) song of the same name, written by [Jim Matheos](https://jimmatheos.com/). I consider it one of his more poetic lyrics. View the [discussion here](/blog/fates-warning-river-wide-ocean-deep/).

I happened upon the artwork to the left while searching for something suitable to use on the site. It sort of represents the merge of music and lyrics to me, plus I just really admire the artist's landscape photography. Visit his site - [Michael Schauer](https://michaelschauer.com/).

Contact me at my site below for any suggestions or recommendations. Enjoy!

~[Michael Kizer](http://michaelkizer.com/)
